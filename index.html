Different Birds:

Potoo




Potoos are a group of Caprimulgiformes birds related to the nightjars and frogmouths.
They are sometimes called poor-me-ones, after their haunting calls.
There are seven species in one genus, Nyctibius, in tropical Central and South America.



Columbidae




Columbidae is a bird family consisting of pigeons and doves.
It is the only family in the order Columbiformes.These are stout-bodied birds with short necks, and short slender bills that in some species feature fleshy ceres.
They primarily feed on seeds, fruits, and plants.
